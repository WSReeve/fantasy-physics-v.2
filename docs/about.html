About

About The Project

Fantasy Physics is a project centered around making Physics education fun, interactive, and accessable. Using online collectables and games, we hope to make physics education intriguing exciting for students of all ages. Additionally, the project supports valuable experiences for the students at KU involved in the maintanence of the educational games and content on the website.

Education and Outreach

Our goal is to introduce physics to a broader crowd of people. By making our content interesting and accessableto people of all ages, we hope to create lots of interest and a bright future for STEM fields.

Who are we?

The Fantasy Physics Team is a group of students and professors at The University of Kansas committed to Physics education and outreach.
